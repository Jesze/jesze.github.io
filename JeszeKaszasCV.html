Additional Background

My main interest since 2006 has been video game production. My first video game project was a video game made with the DOOM engine that told a story in the Halo universe. This project gave me the desire to push game engines to their limit, in this case making a fully 3D Halo style game with a primarily 2.5D engine. I also worked on several smaller scale DOOM projects. All of these taught me the basics of 3D rendering, as well as level design and cutscene design.

When I became co-founder of Forest Bear Studios I learned the value of teamwork in video game production. Our unreleased game project, Univursa, was a traditional RPG game with an expansive stylized world where you played as a sentient bear. This game taught me the process of creating a 2D game engine from scratch using only C++ code. I also was tasked with the design/programming of a Game Editor to supplement our custom engine which taught me valuable interaction design skills. Forest Bear Studios also has a YouTube channel with over 23,000 subscribers where we make entertainment based video game content and Magic the Gathering gameplay videos. To date I have edited/produced/starred in over 1200 videos which have garnered nearly 5,000,000 views. 

While working at Neospatial I worked on a variety of virtual reality based projects for the HTC Vive.

As co-founder and Technical Artist/Lead Programmer of Simulacrum Interactive I have worked on multiple VR demos, including a swimming simulation, a VR based archviz with interactive kitchen elements, and a VR advanced particle effect demo. I also worked on a VR platformer project called Etherian which launched in Early Access on Steam on May 26, 2018.

My hobbies include music production, as well as playing guitar,bass,harmonica and synthesizer.
