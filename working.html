ABOUT US

Simulacrum Interactive is a company of content creators based in Lethbridge, Alberta who know that the future waits for no one, and that the future of digital media is in the changing ways that we interact with it. Experiencing is the new believing, and at Simulacrum Interactive we use cutting edge technology to hand craft immersive, creative, and exciting experiences.


From marketing and promotional applications, to education, prototyping, entertainment and more- we work with you to make your ideas a reality!
