ABOUT US

Simulacrum Interactive is a company of content creators based in Lethbridge, Alberta who know that the future waits for no one, and that the future of digital media is in the changing ways that we interact with it. Experiencing is the new believing, and at Simulacrum Interactive we use cutting edge technology to hand craft immersive, creative, and exciting experiences.


From marketing and promotional applications, to education, prototyping, entertainment and more- we work with you to make your ideas a reality!

Virtual Reality

Using the latest in VR technology and peripherals, we create tailor-made immersive experiences.

Imagine walking through your new home, testing out flooring and paint colors before it’s even built, or learning how to monitor your gages and keep your bearings as you dive deep into the sea from somewhere warm and dry - VR has the power to put you there.

From accessible portable viewers that require only a smartphone to use to immersive room-scale setups that are fully interactable, ask us how we can turn your ideas into reality.

Visualization & Interactive Media

Bringing your visualizations to the next level!

Using powerful game engine technology, we take 3D renders to the next level by merging cutting edge graphics with real time simulations and interactivity.

Whether you’re showcasing a new product or looking to visualize data in an engaging way, we’ll work with you to create content that goes beyond an image. From accessible portable viewers that require only a smartphone to use to immersive room-scale setups that are fully interactable, ask us how we can turn your ideas into reality.

Game Development

Games made for VR.

Game developers at our core, we at Simulacrum strive to create exciting, fun, and unique experiences that are only possible in virtual reality.

If you would like to hear more about our current and upcoming titles or would like to have us develop a game for you, let us know!
